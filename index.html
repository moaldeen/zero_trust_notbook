A Zero Trust Architecture for Secure Connected and Autonomous Vehicles

Welcome to our exploration of zero trust architecture in the context of connected and autonomous vehicles. As the automotive industry evolves towards more connected and autonomous systems, ensuring the security and integrity of these vehicles becomes paramount.

Our zero trust approach considers every component, connection, and data exchange as potentially compromised, implementing rigorous authentication and authorization at every step.

Key Principles:

  • Continuous authentication and authorization
  • Least privilege access
  • Microsegmentation of vehicle systems
  • End-to-end encryption for all data in transit
  • Real-time threat detection and response
